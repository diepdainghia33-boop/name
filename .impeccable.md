## Design Context

### Users
Operators, analysts, and builders who use the app as an AI chat workspace plus dashboard. They need to move quickly between conversation, system status, analytics, and settings on desktop-first sessions.

### Brand Personality
Exact, calm, technical, and deliberate. The interface should feel like a well-run command center: confident without being flashy, premium without being decorative, and clear under pressure.

### Aesthetic Direction
Dark editorial command center with graphite surfaces, warm brass accents, and restrained motion. Avoid neon glows, cyan-on-dark, purple-blue gradients, and generic glassmorphism. Use crisp hierarchy, strong spacing rhythm, and typography that feels intentional rather than default.

### Design Principles
- Keep hierarchy obvious at a glance.
- Use one restrained accent color for emphasis, not decoration.
- Prefer solid surfaces and subtle borders over glow-heavy effects.
- Make chat, dashboard, and settings feel like parts of one system.
- Keep mobile usable without hiding primary actions.
